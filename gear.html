Recommended Hiking Gear

Must-haves

  • 1L water/hr
  • Full lunch
  • Backpack
  • Clothing Layers
  • Sunscreen
  • Cell phone
Hiking poles are a great tool.
I highly recommend a hydration backpack. Fill it with water, throw in your lunch and snacks, maybe an extra pair of socks...and you're golden!
Hiking boots aren't a necessity to get outside, but they are nice to have if you encounter mud, water, and slippery sand.
